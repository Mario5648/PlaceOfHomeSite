Our Mission



"Help our users find a place to call home"




We don't just want our users to find the "box" to call home - we want to help our users find the area to call home. The area in which you live - will determine your commute times to your nightlife. We aim to present as much information as possible when searching a zip code to help you choose your next home - all while making the process easier and limiting the surprises when you arrive.

With many factors like remote work and cost of living playing a role where one lives, people will move to cities and sometimes states they are unfamiliar with. While searching for your next home, one may wonder what the area is like. Such as: how safe is the area? How long does it take to commute? What is the popular commute method? What is the weather like during the seasons? What is the cost of living? What is nearby? Are some questions people may have when searching for a home, and we aim to answer all those questions and much more.
